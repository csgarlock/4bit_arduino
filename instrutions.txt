0x0 store next 4 bits to A
0x1 move A to B
0x2 move B to A
0x3 move B to *A
0x4 move *A to B
0x5 NULL
0x6 jump to B if A is zero
0x7 Output B to *A of LED Red
0x8 Output B to *A of LED Green
0x9 Output B to *A of LED Blue
0xA Store A + B to A
0xB Store A - B to A
0xC Store A and B to A
0xD Store Not A to A
0xE Store Not B to B
0xF Store A xor B to A
